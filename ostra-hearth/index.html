Packing & Safety
  • Always include an ice pack. Pre-warm thermos with boiling water for hot dishes.
  • Grapes must be halved lengthwise. Cherry tomatoes halved. No large round foods.
  • These recipes are nut-free — sunflower butter replaces peanut butter throughout.
  • Aim for: protein + whole grain + fruit/veg + optional dairy at every lunch.

Help

How do I build my weekly plan?
Go to Weekly Plan and tap any empty slot — a recipe picker will appear. You can also type a meal name directly, or tap Surprise Me for a random week.
How does the shopping list work?
Once you've built your plan, go to Shopping List. All ingredients from your chosen recipes are automatically combined, de-duplicated and sorted by category. You can also add items manually.
What is BLW?
Baby-Led Weaning — an approach to introducing solids where babies feed themselves from the start. The BLW section has recipes for every developmental stage from 6 months to 2 years.
How do I save a recipe to My Cookbook?
Open any recipe and tap ✓ Made It. It saves to My Cookbook where you can rate it and view it anytime. You can also add your own recipes from the My Cookbook tab.
How do I change my family settings?
Tap the family badge (the pill showing your family size) at the top of any page to reopen the setup screen.
How do the Instacart and Amazon Fresh buttons work?
Once your shopping list is generated, tap Shop on Instacart or Shop on Amazon Fresh. Your ingredients are passed to the retailer's site for easy ordering.
Something's not working
Tap the Share Feedback button below and describe what's happening — we'll look into it right away.
